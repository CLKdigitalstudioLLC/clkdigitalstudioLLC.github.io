Privacy Policy

Overview

CLK Digital Studio LLC builds Android applications designed to respect user privacy. This policy applies to all apps published under the CLK Digital Studio LLC developer account on Google Play.

Data We Collect

All apps are designed to store data locally on your device only. CLK Digital Studio LLC does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information on external servers.

  • All app data is stored on your device
  • No account or login is required
  • No data is shared with any third parties

Third-Party Services

Apps use Google Play Services for distribution and in-app purchases. Google's privacy policy governs their data practices. No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services are integrated.

Permissions

Some apps may request device permissions (such as camera or storage) solely to enable features the user chooses to use. These permissions are never used for data collection.

Children's Privacy

Apps published by CLK Digital Studio LLC are not directed at children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
